Who am I ?

Music Producer / Pianist

Hello! I'm Harold Justiniano, GH4ROLD as an artist, a music producer born in Bolivia and raised in Chile. From a very early age, music has been an important part of my life thanks to the influence of my family, and I grew up learning to play the piano. I am currently studying music arts pedagogy at the Universidad Mayor, and my passion has always been teaching and producing music. I am passionate about helping others find their creative voice and express themselves through music, and have always looked for ways to share my love of music with others. As a music producer, I have worked on a wide variety of projects. I'm always looking for ways to experiment and explore new sounds and styles, and my goal is to create music that connects with people's emotions and experiences.
